Hangoutville is a platform to connect directly with other people in your city.

How it works
  1. Create a post in your city introducing yourself
  2. People reply directly to your inbox
  3. Respond to replies or discard them
Why this platform?

Hangoutville solves the problem of not knowing much about people in our city. We can get to know people in a casual manner, without consuming lots of our time.

Often, we are surrounded by 1,000s (if not 1,000,000s!) of people, yet with no quick introduction to them, we'll never know if we want to meet them or not.

Hangoutville solves that by letting people briefly introduce themselves to their city.

How can I use Hangoutsville?

Here are some examples to get your mind kickin':

  1. Find a piano teacher
  2. Offer to teach guitar
  3. Find collaborators for a project
  4. And of course, make friends!
